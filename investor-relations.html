Why We Chose Blockchain for OSCORP Tokens

Decentralization: By utilizing blockchain technology, our tokens are decentralized, meaning they are not controlled by any single entity, providing greater resilience and security against manipulation or censorship.

Security: Blockchain technology offers robust security features, such as cryptographic encryption and distributed consensus mechanisms, ensuring the integrity and immutability of transaction records.

24/7 Markets: Unlike traditional stock exchanges with limited trading hours, blockchain-based markets operate 24/7, allowing investors to buy, sell, and trade OSCORP tokens at any time, increasing market accessibility and liquidity.

Transparency: Transactions on the blockchain are transparent and verifiable by anyone, enhancing trust and accountability in our token ecosystem. Investors can track token movements and verify ownership without reliance on intermediaries.

Liquidity Pool Fees: When trading OSCORP tokens on decentralized exchanges (DEX), fees collected from swaps contribute to the liquidity pool, enhancing market liquidity and potentially strengthening the value of the Oscorp token over time.
